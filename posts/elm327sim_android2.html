Enhance simulating accuracy with lua



Now you can enhance the accuracy of your car simulation by using Lua scripts On the sim page of the app



Then edit or copy paste your script in the editor, and save it. The script will be executed every time a command is sent to the simulator, allowing you to customize the responses based on the commands received. The ELM327 layer is already handled by the app, so you can focus on simulating the vehicle behavior and responses to OBD-II/UDS/custom commands.



As always you can check result of your sim in the log page and see the commands sent and responses received in real time.
