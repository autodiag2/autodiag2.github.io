Simulating a car from your Android phone

You can simulate a car directly from your Android phone using the ELM327SimAndroid application.

Download the application from GitHub releases and install it on your device.

Once installed, start the simulator. The application runs a virtual ELM327 interface on your phone, emulating a vehicle ECU and standard OBD-II responses.

You can then connect your preferred diagnostic tool to the simulator, exactly as if you were connected to a real car. For example, you can use AndrOBD , an Android OBD diagnostic application compatible with ELM327 devices.

This setup is useful for testing diagnostic applications, developing scantools, or experimenting with OBD-II protocols without requiring physical vehicle hardware.
