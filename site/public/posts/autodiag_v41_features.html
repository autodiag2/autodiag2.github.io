Autodiag v41

Thanks to signals, an abstraction layer over the diagnostic data, anything built ontop of libautodiag has capabilities to send, generate and receive signal below is an example use case with sim generator gui.

Launched with: 

        ./output/bin/elm327sim -g gui
        ./output/bin/autodiag gui -n /dev/ttys001
This will allow to feed the lib with time series, improve the android app and maybe in the future support CAN signals with DBC format.
